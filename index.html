Welcome to Thengisangaye Primary School

Providing quality education to shape bright future
Our school was established in year 2000 in Thokoza area in Ulundi. The first principal of our school was Mrs M Sikhosana who retired in 2014. Our school started with grade 1 and only two teachers and it grew over years.

Thengisangaye Primary School
